About

A Full-Service Technology Company

Welcome to the home of Tech Tripp, LLC, a full-service technology company serving the Tampa Bay and surrounding areas with managed technology services, server management, IT support, consulting, graphic and web design, network design and professional photography services. We have been serving businesses in the Tampa Bay area for over 10 years. From full vCIO/CTO, managed services, server management and IT support, to graphic and web design, our team is adept at crafting efficient and successful solutions that are custom-designed for each business.

Tech Tripp is owned and operated by Jason Tripp, Esq. Jason has been in the MSP, consulting and IT provider industry for over 15 years. Jason’s approach with Tech Tripp has been simple – starting first with client relations. Tech Tripp treats every client with respect and courtesy. We develop trusting and long-term relationships with our clients. We are not some fly-by-night IT provider. We are a recognized name as a premier IT solutions provider in greater Tampa Bay area. Please review our specific services list to the right for more detailed info on each of our services that we offer.

Not Your Typical IT-Servcie Provider

The selection of your technology and information solutions provider can be challenging for any business. There are literally hundreds of companies to choose from. Tech Tripp focuses their efforts on small to medium sized business (SMB). Small to medium sized businesses have specific needs when it comes to information technology. It’s obvious that every small to medium sized business desires enterprise level support, redundancy and performance but without the “enterprise” cost!

Tech Tripp understands what it takes to run an SMB, because they are one, and they provide technology solutions that work within the clients’ budgets. We are not just another IT solutions provider. Our approach is not to give you a canned set of hardware and software, put you in long term contracts, but rather, by constantly researching and testing the ever changing array of software and hardware available, we provide you with options that are rigorously tested and ready for business use.

Every Client is Unique and We Understand That

Every client of Tech Tripp begins with an audit of the organization’s existing systems. Next, we document our findings and begin our recommendations with a triage approach. That is, the most important areas of concern are addressed first. We then implement affordable functional technology solutions which are proactively monitored and maintained. Our goal is to standardize and enhance your system. We are a company that innovates, thinks and is constantly staying in front of the next “big thing. You can rest assured that with Tech Tripp, you don’t have worry about your IT. We manage your technology every step of the way so you can get back to your business.
